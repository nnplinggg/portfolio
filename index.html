Hi, I'm Linh.

Hi, I'm Linh — born on Christmas, in Quang Ninh Province, Vietnam. I grew up building cardboard houses for stray cats and helping Santa run imaginary classes for Teddy and Barbie. Somewhere between those sketchy Math formula and a fantasy about delivering books to every child on earth with Santa Claus, I figured out what I actually wanted to do with my life. I want to be a builder, one that bridges the distance between people and what they deserve access to. I don't separate strategy from people. Every system — a product, a program, a community — succeeds or fails based on how well it understands real human pain. That belief took me from founding youth programs in my province, to doing gap analysis for neurodivergent teen artists in San Francisco, to building AI agent for early-stage founders or teacher allocation optimization tool, and many more. I'm currently at Minerva University, rotating through 4 continents in 4 years, studying CS and Business, and trying to stay as close to real people as possible while I learn to build bigger systems. Long term? I'm not optimizing for a title. I believe in my ability to connect us humans using data, technology, and stories — and eventually, maybe, go back to host that Barbie class for kids in the mountains.
