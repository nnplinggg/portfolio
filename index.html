About me

Hi, I’m Linh — born on Christmas, in Quang Ninh, Vietnam.

I grew up building cardboard houses for stray cats and helping Santa host imaginary classes for Teddy and Barbie.

Now, I want to be a Product Manager who bridges the distance between people and what they deserve access to.

As a dreamer and leader, I build products and communities that connect us, humans, through data, technology, and stories — from a youth entrepreneurship club to AI-powered tools for urban planners, strategists, and educators.

Currently CS and Business double major at Minerva University, where I’ll rotate through 4 continents and continue my building journey — and eventually, maybe, go back to host that Santa class for kids in the mountains.
